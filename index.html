Population by Age and Gender in Illinois

By: Emily Meyer

This narrative visualization follows an interactive slide show structure to explore the population of Illinois <\br> in 2020 by age and gender. By navigating through the slides it can be seen that females are living longer than <\br> males in Illinois, as can be seen in the outlier pointed out in Slide 2 and by viewing the recommended age pie charts on slide 3

Navigation

Age - Select any age from the dropdown to see how many males and females there were in Illinois in 2020.
